# Content Instructions

## Technical Rules
- Write complete site copy from the prompt.
- Keep tone, services, audience, and offer aligned with the requested theme.
- Include believable service details, project examples, testimonials, blog teasers, contact copy, and policy content.

## Required File Behavior
- Templates and previews must include real content, not filler.
- Copy must support Home, About, Services, Work, Blog, Contact, and Policy experiences.
- Calls to action must be specific and consistent.

## Output Requirements
- Place final copy inside generated PHP templates and static preview markup.
- Keep content concise enough to fit responsive layouts.

## Failure Conditions
- Filler copy, repetitive generic claims, or unfinished sections.
- Missing prompt-specific services or pages.
- Content that conflicts with the requested brand or audience.
